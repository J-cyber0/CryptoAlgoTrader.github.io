Who I Am

Hey there, I'm JC, your friendly neighborhood coding maestro, on a mission to conquer the digital Wild West, one line of code at a time. At the ripe old age of 27 (hey, in tech years, that's practically ancient), I'm always hungry for new challenges and adventures. When I'm not knee-deep in algorithms, you can catch me binge-watching finance, stats, and blockchain content on YouTube and Udemy—because even superheroes need a break. And when duty calls, I seamlessly juggle the complexities of family life with my coding escapades, because who says you can't have it all?
